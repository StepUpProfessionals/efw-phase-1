Weekly Structure

How the Weekly Activation Works

  1. 1 Study Core Expressions
  2. 2 Complete Skill Activation
  3. 3 Take the Quiz
  4. 4 Prepare the Practice Prompt
  5. 5 Attend the Live Speaking Lab
  6. 6 Apply and Reinforce
Each week follows the same structure. Progress depends on consistency.
Course Map

Your 4-Week Progression

Week 1

Structural Foundation: Introducing Yourself

Focus Build a clear and simple professional introduction
Lab Goal Introduce yourself using short structured sentences
Enter Week 1
Week 2

Functional Construction: Talking About Your Role

Focus Ask and answer basic questions about your work
Lab Goal Maintain a short structured Q&A exchange
Enter Week 2
Week 3

Speaking Activation: Building Flow

Focus Connect short responses with more clarity and less hesitation
Lab Goal Maintain a brief professional exchange using 2–3 connected sentences
Enter Week 3
Week 4

Integration: Structured Professional Exchange

Focus Combine all previous structures into one short interaction
Lab Goal Deliver a clear and structured 60–90 second professional exchange
Enter Week 4
Expectations

Your Commitment

  • Complete the weekly platform content before the live sessions
  • Review and reuse the Core Expressions from each week
  • Practice aloud, even if only for a few minutes each day
  • Participate actively in the live sessions
  • Move through the program in sequence without skipping steps
Progress happens through structure, repetition, and guided use.
